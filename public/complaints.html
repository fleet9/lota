How It Works

Filing a complaint takes about two minutes.

1

Tell us what happened

Select the type of complaint and describe the incident in your own words — route, vehicle and date if you remember them.

2

Leave a contact number

A phone number is required so the association can follow up with you if more detail is needed.

3

Submit

Your complaint is logged directly with the association for review.

4

Reviewed by leadership

Complaints are reviewed and, where appropriate, escalated into formal disciplinary cases against the driver or vehicle owner involved.
